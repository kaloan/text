conjugation - defines endings when conjugating in various persons/tenses/moods
3rd conjugation iō - verbs that have an -iō ending in 1st person singular and are conjugated more similarly to 4th conjugation verbs
anomalous - have specific conjugations that can be completely different from the typical
deponent - have passive form for conjugating but are actually active
semi-deponent -like standard when the tense is nonperfect, and like deponent when it is 
transitive - have supin
intransitive - lack supin
impersonal - have only 3 person singular version
defective - ???
